American Cyborg - Steel Warrior

Titel: American Cyborg - Steel Warrior 
 I don't know what gets into me sometimes. When I rented this film in my local videostore, I got the impression that it was Cyborg 2. So what? Blow me. Everbody is entitled to do mistakes, even me. I really like the post-apocalypse films. Mad Max 1-2, Hardware, Bronx Warriors or even 2019-After the Fall of New York. But this one really sucked! Strangely enough it has it all, cyborgs, cannibal mutants, action, a large chested heroine and a noble as hell hero. The story: Women can't have babies. Mary can. She must get to Europe to raise the snotty little thing. She must get through the city alive first. A cyborg is at her tail. A hero Austin (Joe Lara from Dr MacQuinn fame) is in the neighberhood and decides to help her. They run for a while. Then they hide for a while. Then they fight karate for a while. Then they run for a while, and it goes on and on and on and on... Sounds like fun kids? It's not. Austin looks like he is more concerned about his looks, Mary from a porno flick and the cyborg. Oh, the cyborg. Gee, this is a real homo-looking gay (pardon the joke!) fully equipped with taight leather jeans and leatherjacket. Schwarzenegger might look cool dressed like that, but John Ryan looks like someone out of the Village People. There are three assets in the film though, two of them belong to the heroine and the third is that Bob Keen and Image Animation did the special effects. But I guess they want to forget that they ever heard about this film. I know I want. What the asshole who directed this mess is called, I didn't bother to check out. Some of you sickos out there might like this mess, but I recommend Nemesis instead.
