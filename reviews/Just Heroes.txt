Titel: Just Heroes

Peter's review of Bullet In The Head has already said everything about the brilliant John Woo, so I go direct to the story! A mob-leader in Hong Kong get's snuffed and everybody who has known him wants now to be the new boss. Friends become enemies and nobody is honest anymore. Can it be that one of them has ordered the execution of the now ex-mobster? Woo is smart enough to give the characters some own life and let them behave like ordinary people does in real life. With one exception though, they all shoot like Clint Eastwood and Charles Bronson rolled into one! Armed with numerous guns, the last half hour of this film is nonstop hardcore action. The Chinese extras leaps out of windows, stairs and some weird daring stunts and insanity. Hope Woo keeps his good work up and doesn't let the American producers hold him down. Why can't Sweden produce films like this? Maybe I should emigrate to China and play roles as an European hitman or something even more vicious. As you don't give a shit what I think, why do you read this review then? See the film, or get shot a hundred times.
