Titel: Night of the Zombies (1980). Publicerad i Violent Vision
aka Virus-Inferno dei morte Viventi
aka Zombie Creeping Flesh
aka Hell of the Living Dead
aka Zombie Inferno
aka Apocalipsis Canibal

Director Bruno Mattei

»What's eating you?« asks one of the characters in this excuse for a movie. Well, what can it be? Let me guess! Bad acting? Lousy dubbing? Lack of script? Pisspoor direction? Bad make-up? 

Director Vincent Dawn (birthname Bruno Mattei and aka Stefan Oblowsky) has made a film full of things you've seen before, but since he obviously forgot his »director's manual« at home, it stinks just like the rotten zombies. 

A toxic gas has leaked out in Africa and sort of »zombiefies« people. A SWAT-team is sent out to the savannah to clean things up. With lots of stock footage of animals and bushes we now know they are in the jungle. Quite clever, huh? Here Bruno has managed to squeeze in a couple of cannibals too just for the good taste (why can't he stick to the point?). 

With a bunch of happy extras who play the zombies (they smile all the time and giggle a little) the rest of the film is a lot of shooting and shouting, with dialogue like »You bastards« and »You damn vegetables«. One good scene exists though. A girl has her eyes poked out from the inside of her head! But that's about it. Really. 

A good soundtrack helps the film a bit. You might remember Goblin's soundtrack from Dawn of the Dead. It's all in here. Bruno, you little thief! Kind of fun all the way, but there is better zombieflicks out there. Check them out instead.
