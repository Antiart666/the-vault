Titel: The Trap

This was a pleasant surprise. When I saw the cover it looked like an obscure little erotic thing. Of course I was right, but the film has more things like a good script and fine actors. Tony (Bird with the Crystal Plumage) Musante plays Michael, a man in his forties who comes face to face with an old love, Marie, splendidly portrayed by Florinda (Flavia the Heretic) Bolkan. Since she has lost him one time before, she intends to keep him all for her self this time. She drugs him and ties him to the the bed. Naked and tied up Michael first believes that she wants to have some kinky sex like they used to have, but soon he realizes that he is trapped. Everything goes on well at the beginning but when Marie's daughter falls in love with Michael, things get out of control. This is a strange little drama with full frontal nudity and strong conflicts. Co-scripted by old fart Lucio Fulci, who used a similar plot in his Devil's Honey. The director Guiseppe Patroni Griffi handles the script very well and the film looks a bit like a theatre play. As a little change of pace from zombies and cannibals, this film is recommended and also has those marvellous Marsillach Sisters, Christina (Opera) and Blanca (Devil's Honey), whose stunning beauty alone makes the money worth spending.
